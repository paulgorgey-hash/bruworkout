Progression Protocol

Weeks 1–4
Learn the movements. Focus on form, depth, and the mind-muscle connection. Use moderate weight.
Weeks 5–8
Add weight every session. Track every lift. Aim for RPE 8–9 on main compounds.
Weeks 9–12
Introduce techniques: pause reps at top of hip thrust, 3-second negatives on RDL.
Weeks 13–16
Deload week at 16 then reassess. By now you will see and feel significant change.
